Siamese cats

Originating from Thailand, the Siamese has been a favorite breed for the Oriental shorthair, Sphynx, and Himalayan.




Persain cats

Hailing from Iran, Persains are known for having Beautiful, long fur coats. Are known to have a flat face compared to most other breeds, and come in almost any color.




Main Coon cats

This cat is known for having a large stature and thick fur coat. It hails from the state of Maine and is the states orricial cat. Are great hunters and were popularized after the CFA recognized them as purebred in the late 1970s.




Ragdoll cats

This breed gets it's name from it's docile temperament. When picked up, they tend to go limp, much like a ragdoll.




Sphynx cats

One of the few cats without fur. The Sphynx are a unique breed in that they are very social, active, and dog-like compared to their contemporaries.
